Jordan Rhea, Systems Consulting

Artisanally, handcrafted systems built specifically for educators.

What I do

  1. Building systems solutions that address specific organizational problems
  2. Data consulting and putting together the tools that small teams of stakeholders can use to make decisions

What I have done

Landing Pages

A web portal connecting students, teachers, and parents to their assignments. Built specifically to solve problems that arose in the confusion during COVID-19 and the asynchronous learning that was taking place.

In order to improve some of the analog systems in Keystone, I built an app that streamlined the weekend checkin and checkout procedure.

Academic Support

A system that allows for a more decentralized organization of the learning lab.

Communication Hub

Due to the size and scope of the Keystone faculty, communication proves to be a difficult system. Building this hub has allowed more departments to create effective communication with the staff.

Six Day Schedule

A tool that builds a teachers 6 day calendar into their Outlook calendars. Also allows for customization and is synched directly to PowerSchool through the magic of code!

Data Tools

Working with the data committee and taking the skills learned from other projects with PowerSchool and Managebac I have developed a system that allows for connecting data directly to dynamic dashboards.

What I will do

Connect the data that teachers are generating each day to the places where it can be used to inform decisions. Assignments, ATLs, Summatives are some of the connections that I have made between PowerSchool/Managebac/Microsoft.

Working with small groups to both develop future tools and use those tools to inform classroom planning and instruction.

Systems Development

  • Extend the communication hub into a whole school tool. Students need a way to connect the many activities and events that they are creating. They also want archives of the previous years extended essays, personal projects, and capstone projects.
  • Take the code from the landing pages, and bring it over to SharePoint. There are lots of uses that have been suggested to me such as a way to connect planning groups to the number of summatives in a cycle and a way for teachers to self-select lessons for observations.

Data Consulting

Using Power BI visualizations of the data in PowerSchool and Managebac. Then we have to draw some conclusions from that data in small inquiry cycles.

Going forward
